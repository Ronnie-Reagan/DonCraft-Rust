Joining the Server

The server should be listed under the Community tab in-game; search for DonCraft.

Alternatively, paste this into the Rust F1 console:

client.connect donmusicstream.duckdns.org:28015 Click to copy

Discord

Join the Discord for updates, community events, and support.

We also use Discord for player reports and admin communication. Not joining may limit your ability to report issues or receive important announcements.

The team frequently discusses potential changes and gathers player feedback there.

Who This Is For

Builders, long-term players, and people who want Rust without constant hostility.

FAQ

  • Is raiding allowed? No. Raiding is not allowed on this server.
  • Is PvP allowed? Yes, but the focus is on community building and cooperation rather than constant warfare.
  • Are there any plugins? Mostly vanilla with minor quality-of-life additions.
  • How is griefing handled? Griefing and toxic behavior are not tolerated. Admins monitor player reports and behavior patterns.
  • How can I contribute? Engage positively, help newcomers, and participate in community events.
